? Warning Signs of "Bad" Choices:

  • Sugar near the top of ingredient list - if listed early, it's a main ingredient.
  • High Sugar, Low Fiber - causes rapid blood sugar spikes.
  • "Healthy" Misconceptions - low fat but very high added sugar is a poor choice.
